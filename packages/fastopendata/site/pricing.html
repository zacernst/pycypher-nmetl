Frequently asked

Pricing FAQ

What counts as a request?

One billable request = one call to /v1/lookup with one address. The batch endpoint counts addresses, not HTTP calls — 1,000 addresses in one batch = 1,000 requests.

Do you cache?

Yes. Identical requests within 24 hours are served from our edge cache and don't count against your quota.

Can I switch plans mid-month?

Up or down, anytime. Upgrades are prorated, downgrades take effect the next billing cycle.

Do you offer a free trial of Pro?

No trial — the free plan gives you the full dataset. If 10k requests aren't enough to evaluate, email us and we'll bump it.

What about overages?

Free overflows return 429. Pro & Business overflows are billed at $0.0002 / request with no cap until your next billing cycle (you can also set a hard limit).

Is there a non-profit / academic discount?

Yes — 50% off any plan for accredited universities, registered 501(c)(3)s, and civic-tech projects. Just email academic@fastopendata.io.
